Design first

We start with how you live: compact layouts, easy cleaning, and materials that stand up to kids, pets, and long work-from-home days.

Our designers pair soft fabrics with solid wood and powder-coated steel for pieces that age well and stay steady.

Responsible materials

We source FSC-certified wood where possible, water-based finishes, and fabrics tested for durability. Offcuts are repurposed into smaller goods to reduce waste.

Local production keeps our footprint low and supports skilled craftsmen across Bangladesh.
