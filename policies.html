Policies

Returns, shipping, privacy, and terms

A quick summary of how we handle your orders, data, and experience.

Returns

Request a return within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Refunds are processed after quality check.

  • Pickup scheduled within 2 business days in Dhaka
  • Store credit or original payment refund
  • Assembly fees are non-refundable once service is completed

Shipping

Dhaka metro: same/next day delivery. Other cities: 2-4 business days. We provide free basic delivery on orders over 5,000 Tk.

  • Tracking shared via SMS and email
  • Optional assembly available at checkout
  • Missed delivery: we retry once at no cost

Privacy

We collect only what we need to fulfill orders, improve support, and personalize offers. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

  • No selling of personal data
  • Cookies used for performance and preferences
  • Access, update, or delete your data by contacting support

Terms

By using our site and placing an order, you agree to our pricing, fulfillment, and warranty terms. Local laws apply for disputes.

  • Prices include VAT unless stated
  • Warranty covers manufacturing defects for 12 months
  • Custom items may have different return eligibility

Email legal@furniture.com or read the full policy PDFs. We are happy to clarify anything before you order.
